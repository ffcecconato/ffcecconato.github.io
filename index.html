About

Flalbert Franklin Cecconato

Is an Italian-Brazilian photographer born in 1985 and currently based in Germany.

He earned a Bachelor degree in Photography from Centro Universitário Senac (São Paulo) in 2017.

Has participated in group exhibitions and contributed to publications in printed and online media, such as Leros Magazine (United Kingdom) and Mídia Ninja (Brazil).
